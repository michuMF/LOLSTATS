WHAT IS LEAGUE OF LEGENDS?

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other’s base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.

WHY LOL IS SO POPULAR

It is a free-to-play game, so anyone can play it. It is easy to learn but difficult to master. It is a team-based game, so players need to work together to be successful. It is constantly updated with new content, such as new champions, maps, and game modes.

LORE

The main storyline of League of Legends centers around the Institute of War, a peacekeeping organization that was founded to prevent conflict between the different regions of Runeterra. The champions of the League are chosen from all over Runeterra to represent their respective regions in battles that are broadcast to the world.

E-sport

The most prestigious League of Legends esports tournament is the League of Legends World Championship. The tournament is held every year, and it attracts millions of viewers from around the world. The 2022 World Championship had a record-breaking prize pool of over $600 million.
